About IETE(ISF)

The Institution of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineers (IETE) founded back in 1953 is India's leading recognised professional society devoted to the advancement in the field of Science and Technology of Electronics, Telecommunication, Computer Science & IT. We at ISF strongly believe that, “Everything Begins with an idea, Ideas provoke imagination. They are the explosion of creativity and innovation!”

    Benefits

  • 1. Can avail the opportunity to visit Industries, academic institutions etc.,
    2. Discounted/Free access to most of the conferences organized by IETE.
    3. Can attend IETE technical programs in India.
    4. Can get access to their courses(employment/startup/Internship) and many more.....
